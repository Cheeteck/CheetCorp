YFAS

YFAS (Your Friends Are Stupid) is a lightweight Windows utility that lets you quickly send random insults or witty phrases anywhere with a simple hotkey press. Perfect for when your friends keep spawn camping, YFAS fetches fresh insults from an online source, copies them to your clipboard, and types them out automatically—saving you the hassle of thinking up comebacks on the spot. Featuring a discreet system tray icon, a customizable cooldown to prevent spam, and easy updates from a remote insults list, YFAS is your go-to tool for injecting humor (or chaos) into your chats, games, or social media.
